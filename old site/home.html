About Us


Jayesh Tex Fab Pvt. Ltd. was established in the year 2008 by Late Shri Vimal Kumar Prajapati. We exports to various countries in Dubai, Behrain, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, U.S.A. , China, Brazil, Oman etc..

Mission

To become a global leader by incorporating highest levels of quality through a combination of technology, service, creative marketing, pricing and cost efficiency.

Vision

To provide continuous value through innovation, expertise, excellence and attain highest level of customer satisfaction.

Our Products

Polyester

Our polyester fabrics are woven under the direction of deft professionals using top quality threads and advance techniques.

Cotton

A natural fiber derived from the seedpod of the cotton plant. and our product is fluffy wadding of a kind originally made from raw cotton.

Rayon

Our rayon fabrics gives same comfort properties as natural fibers, and it assures in compliance with international quality parameters.
